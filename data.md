## Project 1: Portraits of Power  
Facial Appearances and the Tacit Domain of Political Selection in China  
with Junyan Jiang  
*American Political Science Review* (Conditionally Accepted)

### Overview

This project examines how perceived facial traits influence elite political promotion and purge risk in China using an AI-based algorithm trained to replicate human facial evaluations at scale.
